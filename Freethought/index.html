Hi there! Welcome to the Tufts University Freethought Society Page!

The freethought movement is characterized by those who encourage the development of opinions and ideas based on science, logic and reason, free from superstition, dogma or belief in the supernatural. We strive to exemplify this movement and to provide like-minded Tufts students a place to belong. In addition, through the use of campus activities and involvement with the campus government and administration we work to continually educate the campus community and enact freethought related changes at Tufts.
